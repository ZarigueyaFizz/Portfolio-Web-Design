About Us

Locally owned, family operated and at your service 52 weeks a year, Lotus Cleaners is dedicated to providing quality work while incorporating ecologically conscious technologies. Our company started as a single washing machine laundry service and has quickly progressed to become the most successful dry cleaning facility in the area. Our downtown location and off-street parking make us the top choice for customer convenience. In the interest of constant improvement, we're adding a coffee bar early next year!

If you have any questions at all about the services we offer, be sure to contact us or stop in during regular business hours to learn more about Lotus Cleaners' unparalleled apparel renewal processes.
